Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai Faculty of Color Network

The Faculty of Color Network (FCON) Database is managed by the Mount Sinai Health System's Office for Diversity and Inclusion (ODI). ODI is committed to creating and sustaining a community for our faculty of color and promoting opportunities for networking and collaboration across the System.

If you have any questions about the FCON Database, please email Ashley Michelle Fowler, Administrative Program Manager, Patricia S. Levinson Center for Multicultural and Community Affairs, Office for Diversity and Inclusion, at ashley.fowler@mssm.edu.

Contact us with any questions.
